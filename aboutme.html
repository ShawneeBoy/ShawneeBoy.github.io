About Me


Hi!


Hey, I'm Shawn. Welcome to my personal portfolio website!

...no, that's a huge overstatement.

Uh... welcome to the thing that is a conglomeration of crudely constructed HTML, CSS, and JS code snippets that I copy-pasted from StackOverflow and Youtube.

I'm not even joking -- that's all that this is. The fancy navbar at the top? That cool typewriter effect? The pretty animations on my projects? That's all just copy-pasted code that I spent hours tweaking.

Then again, maybe that's really all coding is.

I'm a freshman at Carnegie Mellon University's School of Computer Science.

I like coding, making music, videography, playing badminton/basketball/guitar/League of Legends (and be terrible at all four), and cultivating deep, trusting relationships with people I like and trust.

Feel free to look around my website, but there's not too much else to see (besides my blog, maybe?). I literally started web development a few weeks ago. So if like my navbar suddenly overlaps elements of my site, or like a heading looks really oddly positioned, I hope you give me a break. This isn't Wordpress or Wix, I'm writing code to try to make this pretty.

Again, welcome. If you've read this far, you get a virtual hug from me. :)
